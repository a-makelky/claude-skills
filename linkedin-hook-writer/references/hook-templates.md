# Hook Templates & Power Phrases

## 5 Hook Types

| Type | Purpose | Example Pattern |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| **How I** | Personal guide on achieving a dream outcome | "How I went from X to Y..." |
| **How to** | Comprehensive guide on achieving a skill/outcome | "How to master X in Y days..." |
| **Start a story** | Inspirational story that motivates or builds authority | "3 years ago, I was broke..." |
| **Captivating quote** | Topic-related quote that resonates deeply | "[Famous quote]. Here's what it taught me..." |
| **Surprising statistic** | Powerful statistic related to the topic | "97% of founders fail at this..." |

## 20 Power Phrases

### Descriptive
Killer, Insane, Ultimate, Secret, Rare

### Actionable
How to, When to, Why you should, If you do this...

### Fear Building
Warning, Stop, Do Not, Attention

### Scarcity/Urgency
Steal, Don't miss, Quickly

### Statistic/Number
10X, 10 [Adjective], The 10 Best

## 10 Hook Templates

1. **Mistakes Template**
   X biggest mistakes [TARGET AUDIENCE] make about [DREAM OUTCOME].

2. **Transformation Template**
   [Time frame] ago, I stopped [COMMON INDUSTRY MISTAKE]. I then achieved [POSITIVE OUTCOME].

3. **Harsh Truths Template**
   10 Harsh [INDUSTRY] Truths After doing [SOCIAL PROOF/AUTHORITY].

4. **Stop/Instead Template**
   Stop [COMMON INDUSTRY MISTAKE]. Instead, [USE MY SOLUTION].

5. **Demystify Template**
   Good [INDUSTRY SKILL] shouldn't be a mystery. It's a skill you can learn.

6. **Statistic + Guide Template**
   People are [CRAZY STATISTIC] after doing [POST TOPIC]. Here is the guide to nailing [POST TOPIC].

7. **Best Lesson Template**
   The best [INDUSTRY] lesson you will receive today: [From someone who has [AUTHORITY/SOCIAL PROOF]].

8. **Quote + Guide Template**
   [SUPER FAMOUS QUOTE ABOUT TOPIC]. Here is the Ultimate guide to [ACHIEVING DREAM OUTCOME].

9. **Reframe Template**
   [TOPIC] is not about [MISCONCEPTION]. It is about [SOLUTION].

10. **Superpower Template**
    The ability to [DO X] is a superpower.
