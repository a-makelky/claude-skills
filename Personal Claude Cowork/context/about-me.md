## Who I Am

Aaron Makelky. Former high school teacher and football coach, 16 years in Wyoming classrooms. Now in SF tech. My entire story is a contrast: rural to urban, education to tech, skeptic to builder.

## What I Do (Personal Brand)

I test AI tools and make them practical for people who don't code and aren't impressed by demos. I create content across LinkedIn, X (Twitter), blog, and short-form video (TikTok, vertical video). I've built an audience of tens of millions of views on TikTok and I'm expanding that presence and skill set across other platforms.

## My Relationship with AI Tools

I use AI as a collaborator, not a ghostwriter. I bring ideas, topics, and rough angles. AI refines, distills, and fits them to my voice. Nothing ever goes out unedited. The better the first pass, the less I need to touch it.

My biggest frustration with AI writing: it defaults to generic, cliché, try-hard LinkedIn influencer tone. It sounds like a gross caricature of real writing. Every output runs through a mandatory ai-writing-audit before it's considered done.
